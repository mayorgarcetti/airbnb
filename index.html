Mayor Garcetti supports allowing Airbnb's with COVID-19 Immune Hosts and Guests

January 27, 2021

Los Angeles Mayor Eric Garcetti

LOS ANGELES — Mayor Eric Garcetti today announced that after a constructive conversation with Joe Gebbia, Co-Founder of Airbnb Inc, he supports waiving the City's current ban on all short term dwelling rentals.

The Mayor stated "Joe's perspective on this was essentially that the science is pretty conclusive on the low risks of COVID-19 immune individuals spreading this disease, and I have to say that I whole heartedly agree."

Ronald Deleo, chief of legislative affairs for the Mayor plans to amend LA Executive Order 1051 to "provide an excemption to the current short term dwelling ban for all persons vaccinated against SARS COVID-19, or who have been infected by natural means and tested positive for antibodies. A fine of $1,000 will by assessed to all persons who falsify such a test or vaccination record."

Disclaimer:
